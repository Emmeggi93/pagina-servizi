🏝️ Capri Group Tour

📝 Tour Description

Once on board, we will depart for Capri and arrive after sailing for approximately 30 minutes. Here you will have about 6 hours of free time to explore the island, discover its characteristic streets, enjoy a lemonade, go shopping or have something to eat. At 3:45 pm you will return on board and the tour will continue sailing around the entire coast of Capri. You will admire a succession of natural beauties such as the Green Grotto, the lighthouse at Punta Carena, the famous Faraglioni rocks, the Natural Arch overlooking the sea and the White Grotto. When returning to the port, we are sure you will already be looking forward to going again!
